Friedrice

How to prepare Fried rice

Ingredients

  • 2 medium chicken thighs deboned & diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic minced
  • 1 small ginger grated
  • 2 tbsp Borges olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 medium onion diced
  • 1 large carrot diced
  • 7 Spring onion chopped
  • 1 medium green bell pepper diced
  • 1 medium red bell pepper diced
  • 1 cup long grain rice cooked
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 egg medium hard boiled
  • 1 tsp black sesame seeds garnish

Preparation

  1. Pour the Borges olive oil into a wok over medium high heat, add the chicken thigh, garlic, ginger, black pepper, soy sauce and stir-fry for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the onion, carrots, stir-fry for a minute then add the spring onion bulb, green bell pepper, red bell pepper and stir-fry for a minute.
  3. Add the cooked rice and toss to mix evenly on high heat.
  4. Add salt, spring onion slices and toss to mix.
  5. Chicken fried rice ready. Serve with split medium hard boiled egg and sprinkle with black sesame seeds.
